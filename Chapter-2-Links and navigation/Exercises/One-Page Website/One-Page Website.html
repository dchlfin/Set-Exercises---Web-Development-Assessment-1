Plot


Mouthwashing follows a crew of five that are adrift aboard the Tulpar, a long-haul freighter for the Pony Express in the vast reaches of space. They are 147 days into their 382-day trip, and a collision in space sees their captain nearly burned alive, and their vessel dead in its tracks.



Key Information

Genre

Mouthwashing is a 2024 psychological horror adventure game developed by Wrong Organ and published by Critical Reflex.

Gameplay

This single-player adventure game is played from a first-person perspective with little in the way of survival or combat mechanics. It can also be described as a walking simulator.

Gameplay primarily involves exploring the Tulpar, engaging in dialogue with the crew, and solving puzzles using items.

Presented as a nonlinear narrative, the game plays out across disjointed scenes taking place in the weeks and months before and following the crash.

Jumps in the timeline are sometimes delineated by non-diegetic transitions that mimic glitches or crashes.

Characters

Curly

Curly is the original captain of the Tulpar, prior to the crash and Jimmy taking over. He was at the top of the ship's command chain and was primarily in charge of navigation.

Anya

Anya is the nurse in charge of the medical supplies, conducting the other crew mates’ psychological evaluations, co-pilot of the Tulpar.

Swansea

Swansea is the mechanic. He is in charge of looking over the ship’s maintenance, ensuring that everything worked accordingly.

Daisuke

Daisuke is an intern working under Swansea.

Polle

Polle is the official Pony Express mascot. He resembles a cartoon horse with a black mane and tail, wearing a Pony Express T-shirt and hat.

Jimmy

Jimmy is the main protagonist and co-pilot of the Tulpar. He is in charge of navigation and taking the captain’s mantle in the event of an emergency.

Pony Express

Pony Express is the overarching antagonist of Mouthwashing. They are an intergalactic delivery corporation whom the Tulpar freighter and its crew perform long-haul deliveries for.
